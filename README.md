## 📋 Overview

<a href="#goProQuik">GoPro Quik</a> serves GoPro camera owners who want to do more with their footage than leave it on the SD card — from casual users who want automatic highlight reels to share with family and friends to active creators who want efficient tools for the action content they produce regularly.

The <a href="#goProQuik">casual GoPro user</a> is best served by the automatic highlight reel feature — importing footage from a trip, ski day, or activity and getting a shareable video without any editing work. The automatic selection, music sync, and assembly produce results that are significantly better than raw footage clips shared individually, with essentially zero editing effort required. The <a href="#goProQuik">active content creator</a> uses Quik's manual editing tools for more controlled output — trimming specific clips, choosing specific moments, applying speed effects, and refining the automatic edit into a polished video that better represents the day's footage.

The <a href="#goProQuik">GoPro ecosystem integration</a> makes Quik the natural management hub for GoPro users — camera firmware updates, settings configuration, and footage management all in one place specifically designed for GoPro hardware. No generic video manager handles GoPro-specific features like HiLights chapters, GoPro metadata, and camera connection as well as the official application. <a href="#goProQuik">Cross-device continuity</a> through the GoPro subscription connects the Mac application with the mobile Quik app — projects and media accessible across Mac and iPhone for editing in whichever context is most convenient.</p>

<a href="#goProQuik">HiLights markers</a> placed during recording on the GoPro camera carry through to Quik — moments the user tagged as highlights during the activity appear in the editing timeline for use in automatic and manual highlight selection. <a href="#goProQuik">360-degree video support</a> handles footage from GoPro MAX and other 360 cameras — viewing, reframing, and exporting 360 content from the Mac desktop.

<a href="#goProQuik">Export quality options</a> produce video at appropriate resolutions for different sharing destinations — social media optimized exports, full quality exports for archiving, and compressed exports for quick sharing without large file sizes. <a href="#goProQuik">Free with GoPro subscription</a> — Quik is available free with full features available to GoPro subscription members, with basic features available without subscription.</p>

Yes. Automatic duplicate detection prevents importing footage already in the library.
